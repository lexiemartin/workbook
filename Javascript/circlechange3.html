Who's afraid of the big bad cool kids?

You might be intimidated by those cool hipster kids. Don't be.

Cool hipster kids are like Siamese cats: they look refined and completely indifferent to your existence. Somehow, their hair is always perfect.

 

The one weird trick hipsters don't want you to know!

People are like cats. Hipsters are like Siamese cats. They seem so impossibly cool and aloof, but really they're just cats. They like naps, food, and lazer pointers. Once you get you know them they're just as weird and awkward as the rest of us.

And once you see the true derpiness that lies beneath the tragically hip facade you'll feel right at home. It'll be just like hanging out with the uncool kids again.

All you have to do is press the Uncool Button.
